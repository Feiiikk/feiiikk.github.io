About this practice

The trackers are not the point.

The point is learning how to design a life I can participate in deliberately. I build when an off-the-shelf app feels too generic, too noisy, or simply not mine.

These projects sit where curiosity meets self-observation: practical enough for Tuesday morning, personal enough to keep changing with me.

BuildingPrivate, cross-device tools that become easier—not heavier—to use.
